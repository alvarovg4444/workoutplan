How This Plan Works

This simplified logger helps you track your maximum weight lifted for key exercises on a 3-day cycle. Aim for progressive overload over time.

Day 2: Bench Press

Train three times per week, focusing on one main lift each day.

Progression

  • When you successfully lift a weight, try to increase it slightly in the next session for that exercise.
  • For lower body lifts (Squat, Deadlift), consider adding 2.5-5 kg.
  • For upper body lifts (Bench Press), consider adding 1-2.5 kg.
  • Listen to your body. Deload or take rest weeks if needed.

Glossary

Max Weight: The heaviest weight you can lift for one repetition with good form.

Warm-up Guide

Always warm up thoroughly before attempting maximal lifts. This typically includes:
5-10 min light cardio.
Dynamic stretching.
Several lighter sets of the main exercise, gradually increasing the weight.

Safety & Recovery

Prioritize good form. Sleep 7-9 hours, eat enough protein, and stay hydrated.
