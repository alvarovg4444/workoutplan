Routine Info

How This Plan Works

Train three times per week. Each day has one “main lift” and supportive work. On your last set, do an AMRAP (as many reps as possible) but stop when form breaks or effort feels like RPE 9.

Progression

  • If last week’s AMRAP felt ≤ RPE 8, add +5 kg to Squat/Deadlift or +2 kg to Bench/Press next time.
  • When progress stalls for two weeks, drop weights to 60 % for one easy week, then continue.

Glossary

Rep: One up-and-down. Set: Group of reps. AMRAP: As many reps as possible. RPE: Effort scale 6 (easy) – 10 (max).

Warm-up Guide

5 min light cardio → empty-bar reps → small jumps to working weight.

Safety & Recovery

Sleep 7–9 h, eat ~2 g protein per kg body-weight, hydrate.
