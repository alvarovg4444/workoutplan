My Workout Plan

Day 1: Lower Body (Squat Focus)

Warm-Up (10–15 min)

  • Dynamic stretches (hip openers, bodyweight squats, leg swings)
  • Barbell warm-up sets for squats (see below)

Main Lift: Back Squat

  • Bar x 10
  • 60kg x 5
  • 90kg x 2–3
  • 4x4–6 @ working weight (e.g. 110kg)

Accessory Lifts

  • Romanian Deadlift – 3x8
  • Walking Lunges – 2x10 each leg
  • Standing Calf Raises – 3x15
  • Hanging Leg Raises – 3x15

Bodyweight AMRAP Finisher

  • Air Squats or Jump Squats – 1xAMRAP (Push your legs with just bodyweight, aiming for 25–50+ reps)

Day 2: Upper Body Push (Bench Focus)

Warm-Up (10–15 min)

  • Arm circles, band work, shoulder mobility
  • Bench press ramp-up: Bar x 10 → 60kg x 5 → 80kg x 2
  • Then: 4x4–6 @ working weight (e.g. 100kg)

Main Lift: Bench Press

  • 4x4–6 @ working weight (e.g. 100kg)

Accessory Lifts

  • Overhead Press – 3x6
  • Incline Dumbbell Press – 3x8
  • Dips – 3x10
  • Triceps Pushdowns – 3x12

Bodyweight AMRAP Finisher

  • Push-Ups – 1xAMRAP (Clean reps, chest touches the floor. Goal: 30–50+ reps)

Day 3: Upper Body Pull (Deadlift + Pull-Up Focus)

Warm-Up (10–15 min)

  • Band rows, scap pull-ups, glute bridges
  • Deadlift ramp-up: Bar x 10 → 100kg x 5 → 140kg x 2
  • Then: 4x4–6 @ working weight (e.g. 160kg)

Main Lift: Deadlift

  • 4x4–6 @ working weight (e.g. 160kg)

Pull-Up Strength Work

  • Weighted Pull-Ups – 4x6 (e.g. +30–40kg)

Accessory Lifts

  • Barbell Row – 3x8
  • Face Pulls – 3x15
  • Hammer Curls – 3x10

Bodyweight AMRAP Finisher

  • Pull-Ups – 1xAMRAP (No weight, clean form. Aim for max reps)
